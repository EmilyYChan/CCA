FAQ

Everything you need to know about CCA.

Who teaches the program?

Our primary instructor is a Harvard Computer Science major with several years of teaching and tutoring experience.

What is the size of the class?

Each session will have 15-20 students, ensuring plenty of individual support for students.

What is the pace of the program?

Intensive. It’s a lot of computer science to pack into one week, and we expect students to stay focused and dedicated to doing their best, regardless of what level they might be at coming into the course.

Python is currently the most popular programming language in the world, and is a general-purpose language, which means it can be used to build just about anything. Uses include machine learning, web programming, data analysis, modelling, scientific computing, and even desktop applications. Google, Youtube, Spotify, Instagram, Dropbox, Quora, reddit, and even games such as Civilization IV and the Sims 4 all make use of Python. Despite being commonly used in the industry, Python is generally not taught in schools—AP Computer Science uses Java, while more basic introductory courses use Scratch.
